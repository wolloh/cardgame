Hearthstone

This is new Hearthstone game where you can fight with someone else
Rules are simple.Each player has 20 cards.Each card has tier from 0 to 4.At the start of game both players takes 3 cards from the top of their deck called "defense"
Then attacking player take one card from top of his deck and choose which card from enemy defense he want to beat
Game continues until one of the players has any card in deck or when someone get fixed value of penalty points
